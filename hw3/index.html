Golden Globe Winners

January 10 2016 marked the 73rd presentation of the Golden Globe Awards. The Golden Globes recognize achievements in 25 categories; 14 in motion pictures and 11 in television.

Winners & Nominees - 2016

This year The Remnant wins big with 3 of the biggest honors: Best Motion Picture-Drama, Best Director - Motion Picture, and with Leonardo DiCaprio winning Best Actor in a Motion Picture-Drama. The Remnant was nominated along with Mad Max:Fury Road, Room, Spotlight and Carol.

2016 Cecil B. DeMille Award

The well-deserved two-time Golden Globe Award winner, Denzel Washington, was honored on Sunday with the 2016 Cecil B. DeMille Award at the “73rd Annual Golden Globe® Awards. The Cecil B. DeMille Award is given annually to a talented individual for outstanding contributions to the world of entertainment.

In Memorium - David Bowie

The news of the famed British rocker, David Bowie's, death was received right after the Golden Globe ceremony and shocked many as they were filing out of the Beverly Hilton Hotel. In celebration of his life and tribute to his acclaimed work, many of the after parties played his songs in remeberance.
